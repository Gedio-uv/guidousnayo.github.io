Self-initiated · AI-assisted · 2026

Products I design and ship myself — with AI as a build partner.

This is where I go past Figma. These are products I take from a raw idea to a live, deployed app — owning the concept, UX architecture, visual identity, and content, then building and shipping them through AI-assisted development.

For me it's less about "using AI" and more about closing the loop. Designing and shipping a real product solo means I validate ideas with real users instead of mockups, make sharper product decisions, and understand exactly what I'm handing to engineering in my day-to-day work.

Each project below started as a personal itch and turned into something real.
